Moonlight Hub

14 / 12 / 2022

Moonlight, a cheat for Roblox, boasts a Discord server with over 5 thousand members! We've been active for approximately 2 years, although we faced a setback when our previous Discord server, boasting 10 thousand members, was terminated. Despite this, we managed to rebuild and regain the script's popularity.

Originally conceived as a script hub where I shared the finest scripts within a single server, Moonlight evolved when @Globe leveraged his expertise to craft the very first Moonlight script.

Currently, we extend support to Blackout: Revival and Criminality, with plans for further expansion and development.

I designed a webpage for Moonlight using Tailwind CSS, aiming for a sleek and minimalist aesthetic. Additionally, a custom dashboard is in the works, enabling users to effortlessly access their account details, keys, HWID, and more.
